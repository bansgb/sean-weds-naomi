How It All Began

Our Story

Naomi Priest is a project manager - a woman who runs marathons, keeps complex systems on track, and radiates calm competence in all things. Sean Gleeson is a brilliant musician and teacher with a huge heart, an infectious laugh, and a gift for making every room he walks into more fun. They met at Leeds Opera - Naomi on stage, Sean conducting - and the rest is history.

After a day of wine tasting in Portugal's Douro Valley, Sean proposed at the top of a gorgeous vista on the 26th of July 2025. Naomi said yes before he'd even finished the question. At least that's how he tells it.
